About Us

At Smokey Cat Cannabis Café, we blend the cozy charm of a cat café with the relaxing benefits of premium cannabis products. Our mission is to create a welcoming space where cat lovers, wellness seekers, and cannabis enthusiasts can unwind together. Founded in Mansfield, OH, we believe in responsible sourcing, legal compliance, and ethical marketing to serve both humans and pets safely.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted cat cannabis café, providing joy, relaxation, and community impact.

Our Values

  • Safety and quality for people and pets
  • Transparency and ethics
  • Community involvement
  • Continuous learning and innovation
