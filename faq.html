Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Cat Cannabis Café?

The Cat Cannabis Café is a unique business combining the relaxing environment of a cat café with the therapeutic benefits of cannabis products. Guests can enjoy cuddles with our resident cats while sampling carefully curated cannabis offerings for humans — plus pet-friendly products for their furry companions!

Why mix cats and cannabis?

In today’s fast-paced world, people seek wellness, stress relief, and unique social experiences. Combining a cat café with cannabis creates a cozy space where visitors can relax, connect, and find companionship — while benefiting from calming cannabis products.

Is it safe for pets?

Absolutely. All pet cannabis products are specially formulated and responsibly sourced. Safety is our top priority: products are tested, properly labeled, and tailored for animals’ unique needs.

How do you ensure legal compliance?

We keep an attorney on payroll to provide ongoing legal guidance. This ensures we follow all state and federal regulations, including licensing, product testing, labeling standards, security, and advertising rules.

What makes your café different from competitors?

Besides the unique concept itself, we offer a dedicated line of pet-focused cannabis products, partner with local animal rescues, and create a welcoming community space. We also invest in employee training to deliver exceptional service.

Do you sell cannabis to everyone?

No — all cannabis sales follow strict age verification and legal purchase limits. Human cannabis products are for adults 21+ with valid ID.

How do you protect customer data?

We use strong cybersecurity measures, modern e-commerce platforms, and a secure CRM system to protect customer information and sales records.

What technologies do you use to run the café?

Our café uses e-commerce for online orders, online booking systems for reservations, and CRM software to track customer interactions — all supported by cybersecurity tools to keep everything secure.

Are your cannabis products ethically sourced?

Yes. We are committed to responsible sourcing and transparency. Our products are tested for safety, and we only partner with reputable suppliers who share our ethical standards.

Do you support local rescues?

Yes! We partner with shelters and private rescues, foster cats waiting for homes, and host adoption events — giving back to the community and helping animals in need.

What is your management philosophy?

We follow Theory Y: employees are trusted, empowered, and encouraged to collaborate. This creates a flexible, organic environment where innovation and customer satisfaction thrive.
